$f: A\to\mathbb{R}$
Allora $m = sup(f) \iff$
1. $f(x) \leq m \;\forall x\in A$
2. $\forall\varepsilon > 0\;\;\exists\:\overline{y}\in f(A)\;\; t.c. \;\;\overline{y}>m-\varepsilon$
